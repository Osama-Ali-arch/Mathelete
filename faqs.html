We're here to help

1 What is Mathlete?

Mathlete is an innovatively designed platform to conduct competitions for young students to develop and boost their interest in different subjects.

2 What is meant by Mathlete's Campaigns?

Mathlete campaigns are the online quiz competitions associated and organized by Mathlete.

3 How can I register for Mathlete campaigns?

Go to Campaigns (on the top menu) -> Choose Campaign (in which you want to participate) -> Click Join (if campaign is open for registration) -> You will be redirected to the respective campaign page to proceed further.

4 Who is Eligible to Participate?

Any young student can register with us to participate in contests associated with Mathlete as per the rules or grades division mentioned for that competition.

5 Is there any specific requirement to enter the contest?

You must be an enrolled student in a Pakistani institute. For small children (grade 3 and onward), their parents can register on their behalf.

6 Do I need to pay for registration?

No, there is no registration fee to join and play Practice quizzes. However, to enter a contest, there might be a fee associated with it.

7 Do I need to register every time for any new contest?

Once you get registered with us for a contest associated with Mathlete, you can participate in any other (or upcoming contests) as per your education level (if allowed in that contest). Choose ‘Already have a Mathlete Account’ option while signing up.

8 How many questions are there in each quiz?

The number and types of questions varies in each quiz.

9 Can I check my results?

Yes, you can check your results from your dashboard (which will be created as soon as you register with us and create an account to participate in Mathlete campaigns)

10 What includes in the analytics report?

The analytics report of a Mathlete contestant is a summary of all the competitions he/she participated in and an overall score. The report will show how well you performed in specific math categories and on the whole, and what areas need improvement. You can get best attempt details, correct answers, overall score and specific category scores (algebra, geometry, etc.)

11 What is meant by ‘Top Mathletes’?

Top Mathletes is the term we use for our Top Contestants or Winners.
